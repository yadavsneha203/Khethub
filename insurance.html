Insurance Policies for Farmers & Buyers

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme that provides coverage against natural calamities, pests, and diseases for farmers.

Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme

A redesigned version of WBCIS offering improved claim settlement process and lower premiums for farmers.

Unified Package Insurance Scheme (UPIS)

Package policy offering crop insurance along with coverage for life, assets, and accidents for farmers.

Buyer-Seller Protection Insurance (Private)

Offered by private insurers, this protects buyers from crop quality issues and farmers from payment defaults.

Crop Loan Insurance Scheme

Loan-linked insurance covering both the crop and repayment risk due to weather failure or market crisis.

Livestock Insurance Scheme

Insurance for farmers to protect their cattle and livestock against death or disease.
